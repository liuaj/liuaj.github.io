Spices

All spices are listed below. They are originally whole and dried unless marked otherwise; I will grind them into powder before delivery if you don't indicate otherwise (in the comments box).

I've tried to base the prices off worldwide unit prices, but the amount I have in stock and the difficulty of procuring a spice may also affect the price. By all means, let me know if you think something is overpriced. The idea is that payments can easily be made with coins.

Spices marked with red are out of stock; please check back later!

Note: I also have peppermint, almond, and vanilla extract, as well as rosewater and orange blossom water. Since those don't package well, I haven't listed them here, but if you need one of them just send me a message and I can meet you whenever you need them.
