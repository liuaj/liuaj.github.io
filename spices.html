Spices

All spices are listed below. They are originally whole and dried unless marked otherwise; I will grind all non-herbs into powder before delivery if you don't indicate otherwise (in the comments box).

Spices marked with red are out of stock or soon-to-be-obtained; please check back later!

Also, there is cinnamon and some powdered sugar in the dhall if you need it. There's a spice rack too, but I'm not sure how often it's refreshed.

Note: I also have peppermint, almond, and vanilla extract, as well as rosewater and orange blossom water. Since those don't package well, I haven't listed them here, but if you need one of them just send me a message and I can meet you whenever you need them.

Warning: Any spice can be dangerous if consumed in large enough quantities. Nutmeg in particular acts as a hallucinogenic drug after one or two tablespoons. It's not a pleasant high, so don't try it. I will question you if you order that much, especially since most recipes require only 1/4-1/2 tsp.
